Bobb-McCulloch

Two distinct buildings join to form Bobb-McCulloch Hall, which is located on the north side of campus. Bobb-McCulloch houses a combined total of 496 students in double- and triple-occupancy rooms. Men and women live in separate wings of the same floor. Residents are close to two of Northwestern's on-campus gyms and Sargent for dining.
